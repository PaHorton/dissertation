When reproducing previously published works as chapters, the original article must be included in its entirety and must retain its original abstract and references within the same chapter. References must also be included within the comprehensive references list for the entire document.
    For the portions of your document that have been previously published, please include an appendix with a citation containing which portions have been previously published, when, and in which journals or books. 
    For previously published works with co-authors, please include signed letters from co-authors granting permission to use the works or a statement indicating that all co-authors have granted their permission.
    Please see ASUs policy regarding Previously Published Works: https://graduate.asu.edu/sites/default/files/policy-on-using-previously-published-work.pdf
